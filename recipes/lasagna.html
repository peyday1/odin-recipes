Quick and Easy Lasagna Recipe

Description

This lasagna recipe is a quick and easy way to enjoy the classic Italian dish in your own kitchen. Made with just a few simple ingredients, including ground beef, pasta sauce, ricotta cheese, eggs, parmesan cheese, mozzarella cheese, and lasagna noodles, this dish is both delicious and budget-friendly. The ground beef is cooked in a large skillet with salt and pepper, then mixed with pasta sauce to form the base of the lasagna. The cheese mixture is made by mixing together ricotta cheese, an egg, parmesan cheese, and seasonings, then spreading it in layers between the lasagna noodles and meat sauce. The lasagna is then baked in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly. This lasagna is perfect for feeding a crowd, or for enjoying as a comforting meal on a weeknight. Serve it hot and enjoy the satisfying taste of this classic dish.

Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 oz) pasta sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe:

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned and crumbled. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Stir in pasta sauce and set aside.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, egg, parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  5. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Place 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, then spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles. Repeat layering with another layer of sauce, noodles, and ricotta.
  8. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly on top.
  9. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  10. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 25 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly. Serve hot.
