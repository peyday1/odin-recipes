Meatball Sub

Description:

The meatball sub is a delicious sandwich made with warm meatballs, marinara sauce, and melted mozzarella cheese served on a toasted sub roll. The meatballs can be homemade or store-bought, and are topped with marinara sauce and cheese before being baked in the oven. The result is a hot, hearty sandwich that's perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients:

  • Meatballs (homemade or store-bought)
  • Sub buns
  • Marinara sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil (optional)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil

Recipe:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Split the sub buns in half and brush with olive oil.
  3. Place the buns on a baking sheet, cut-side up, and bake for 5 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, warm the marinara sauce in a saucepan over low heat.
  5. Place the meatballs on the bottom half of each sub bun.
  6. Spoon the warm marinara sauce over the meatballs.
  7. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  8. Return the subs to the oven and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove from oven and let cool for a few minutes.
  10. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil, if desired.
